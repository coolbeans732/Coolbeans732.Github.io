Start Your Day with a Devotional

Spend a few quiet minutes with God each day. Below are some devotionals to uplift your spirit and strengthen your walk.

Peace in the Storm

Philippians 4:7 — "And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus."

No matter what you're facing, God’s peace is real. Take a breath today and let His calm guard your thoughts.

God is Near

Psalm 145:18 — "The Lord is near to all who call on him, to all who call on him in truth."

Even when you feel far from God, He’s listening. Talk to Him, and trust His closeness.
