Start Your Day with the Word

Here you’ll find short, uplifting devotionals to guide your day.

Trusting God's Timing

Verse: Ecclesiastes 3:1 — "There is a time for everything, and a season for every activity under the heavens."

Reflection: Sometimes we rush God’s plan, but He always works in perfect timing. Let’s trust His schedule and not our own.

Peace Over Worry

Verse: John 14:27 — "Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you."

Reflection: Worry adds weight to our souls. Give your burdens to Jesus today and choose peace over panic.
